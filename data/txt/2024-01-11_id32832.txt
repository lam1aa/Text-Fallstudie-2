Aus diesem Anlass möchten wir darüber diskutieren, wie sich die demokratischen Kräfte für das erneute “Super-Wahljahr” aufstellen können. Kann es noch gelingen, die Wahlerfolge der extremen Rechten in den ostdeutschen Bundesländern zu verhindern? Was erwartet uns auf europäischer Ebene? Und was hat das Alles mit Treptow-Köpenick zu tun? Cake*Talks – Cake meets theory and practice | Topic “Elections 2024” Friday, 12.01.2024, 5 pm Zentrum für Demokratie, Michael-Brückner-Straße 1, 12439 Berlin The election year 2024 will be upon us on 12 January. In addition to the European elections and the state elections in Saxony, Thuringia and Brandenburg, around 1/4 of Berlin’s polling stations are now also due to hold a repeat of the Bundestag elections. On this occasion, we would like to discuss how the democratic forces can position themselves for another “super election year”. Can we still succeed in preventing the electoral successes of the far right in the eastern German states? What can we expect at European level? And what does all this have to do with Treptow-Köpenick? We invite you to discuss and eat cake together at Michael-Brückner-Straße 1 (extension to the Bürgeramt, right next to Schöneweide S-Bahn station). New snacks every month: Cake and theory at the Treptow Köpenick Centre for Democracy. Chatting: about studies on the extreme right, racism and anti-Semitism | Reflection: What does this have to do with us? What can we do?
