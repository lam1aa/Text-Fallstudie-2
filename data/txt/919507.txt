Mittwoch, 14:00 – 16:00 Uhr Familienleben Sprachen: Deutsch, Englisch, Französisch, Arabisch … auf einmal ist die ganze Familie zu Hause. Mittwoch, 17:00 – 18:00 Uhr Technik, Technik, Technik Sprachen: Deutsch, Englisch, Französisch Wie funktionieren Video Chats, Messengerdienste oder Apps wie Trello und co? Donnerstag, 13:00 – 14:00 Uhr Märchenstunde Sprachen: Deutsch, Arabisch Asya und Evy laden euch herzlich zur magischen arabisch/deutschen Märchenstunde ein. Haltet Malpapier und Stift bereit und lasst beim Zuhören magische und phantasievolle Bilder entstehen! Donnerstag, 17:00 – 18:30 Uhr Online PfD-Sprechstunde: Politisch aktiv – aber wie? Samstag, 10:00 – 12:00 Uhr Deutsch-Frühstück Sprachen: Deutsch Hier treffen sich Menschen, die sich auf Deutsch unterhalten wollen. Du brauchst: Ein Getränk und dein Lieblingsfrühstück. Wochenprogramm 14.- 18.4.2020: Digitales Sprachcafé | Digital Language Café PDF-Dokument (2.1 MB) Download DIGITAL LANGUAGE CAFÉ: APRIL 14 TO APRIL 18 Taking part is easy! For smartphones you need the zoom app. But you do not have to register! We will send you a link that explains everything else. Contact us here or send an e-mail to interaxion@offensiv91.de . Tuesday, 11:00 – 12:30 H Dancing and Yoga for women only Languages: German, English, Turkish, French, Arabic Nergis invites you to dance with her. This video chat is for women only! If you want to participate, write us an email: interaxion@offensiv91.de . Tuesday, 14:00 – 15:30 H How to … story telling? Languages: German, English, French, Hebrew With Kim and Guy we write a story together and visualize it in a picture. How does it work? Envision a bit of chaos, creativity and lots of fun. Tuesday, 17:00 – 19:00 H Speed dating f the open neighbourhood Languages: German, English, French During our virtual speed dating we get into conversation with short games to discover common ground, exchange opinions and ideas. As a small group we all talk together. With many participants, we form small groups. Wednesday, 14:00 – 16:00 H Family Life Languages: German, English, French, Arabic .. suddenly the whole family’s home. In this digital language café, Basti and Katja from the NBZ in Friedrichshagen want to talk about the situation at home. Wednesday, 17:00 – 18:00 H Digital tools and how they work Languages: German, English, French How do video chats, messenger services or apps like Trello work and what’s their use? Together we get to know digital programs (tools) and how we can use them for a digital activities. Wednesday, 19:00 – 20:00 H Shared online dinner Languages: German, English Normally the welcome office and Über den Tellerrand invite to community cooking events in Schöneweide. Since that’s not possible now, we eat together digitally. We talk about our everyday life and play a small game each week, e.g. “Stadt, Land, Fluss” Thursday, 11:00 – 12:00 H What’s your favourite book? Languages: German, English, Spanish A lot of people are reading books these days. Let’s share our favourite texts, books, articles. We are looking forward to your inspirations. Thursday, 13:00 – 14:00 H Fairy tales Languages: German, Arabic Asya and Evy warmly invite you to the magic Arabic/German fairy tale hour. Have drawing paper and pencil ready and let magical and imaginative pictures arise while listening! Thursday, 13:00 – 14:00 H Political action online – how does it work? Languages: German, English Claudia and Katja from the Partnerships for Democracy want to talk to you about it, how to get involved in politics online now and how to create your own project ideas developed. Saturday, 10:00 – 12:00 H German Breakfast Languages: German People meet here who want to talk in German. All you need: A drink and your favourite breakfast. We are looking forward to seeing you! To participate, please contact bianca@die-lernwerkstatt.org . Inspiration for personal activities Every week, Nergis, Louisa and other creative people share videos for the weekend on relaxation exercises, gardening at home and DIY instructions and much more. You can get in touch here: http://tueroeffner-ev.de/kontakt/ or send an sms to 017649652827. They are also available from Monday to Friday, 13.00 to 17.00 h by telephone.
